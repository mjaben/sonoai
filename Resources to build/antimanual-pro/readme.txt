== Description ==

**Transform your WordPress site with Antimanual Pro, the all-in-one AI powerhouse.**

Antimanual Pro isn't just a plugin; it's your intelligent assistant. It seamlessly combines a smart Chatbot, Auto-Posting, Documentation Generator, Bulk Rewrite tools, bbPress Forum Automation, and AI Search to revolutionize how you manage your site.

Whether you're a business owner looking to reduce support costs, a content creator needing to scale production, or a developer wanting to streamline documentation, Antimanual is the solution.

### Key Features

*   **Intelligent AI Chatbot**
    Train a custom chatbot on your own content—posts, pages, PDFs, and URLs. It delivers instant, accurate answers to your visitors, reducing support tickets and boosting engagement. Now with GPT-5 support!

*   **Auto-Posting & Content Creation**
    Put your content strategy on autopilot. Schedule and automatically publish fresh, SEO-optimized posts based on your chosen topics and tone. Keep your blog active without lifting a finger.

*   **AI Documentation Generator**
    Instantly generate comprehensive, clean documentation. Select your language, tone, and structure, and let the AI build professional docs for your products or services.

*   **Bulk Rewrite & SEO Optimization**
    Refresh your existing content in bulk. Rewrite posts to improve readability, update information, or target new keywords. Automatically generate SEO-friendly excerpts, tags, and terms.

*   **Forum Automation**
    Breathe new life into your forums. Automatically convert closed topics into helpful knowledge base posts and generate smart AI replies to keep discussions moving.

*   **Smart Knowledge Base**
    Build an organized knowledge system by converting your existing content into structured, searchable resources. Supports PDFs, URLs, and custom text.

*   **AI-Powered Search**
    Upgrade your site's search experience. The customizable AI search block understands user intent and delivers relevant results, complete with editable text and icons.

== Installation ==

1.  **Upload**: Upload the `antimanual-pro` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2.  **Activate**: Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3.  **Configure**: Go to **Antimanual > Settings** in your dashboard.
4.  **Connect**: Add your OpenAI API key to enable the AI features.
5.  **Enjoy**: Start leveraging AI-powered features!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Antimanual? =
Antimanual is a comprehensive AI plugin for WordPress that offers features like a custom chatbot, auto-posting, documentation generation, and more to automate and enhance your website.

= Do I need an OpenAI API key? =
Yes, Antimanual uses OpenAI's powerful models (like GPT-4 and GPT-5) to function. You will need to provide your own API key in the settings.

= Can I train the chatbot on my own data? =
Absolutely. You can train the chatbot using your website's posts, pages, custom text, PDFs, and even external URLs.

= Is the Auto-Posting feature fully automated? =
Yes! Once you set your topics, tone, and schedule, Antimanual will generate and publish posts automatically according to your plan.

= Does it work with bbPress? =
Yes, we have deep integration with bbPress. You can automate replies to forum topics and convert solved topics into permanent documentation or blog posts.

= Is there a premium version? =
Yes, while the free version is powerful, Antimanual Premium offers advanced features and higher limits. Check the "Useful Links" section for more info.
